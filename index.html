About Me

Hello! My name is Trevor Loe and I am a Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics at UCLA. I graduated as a double major in Physics and Mathematics, Summa Cum Laude at the California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I am generally interested in research related to numerical linear algebra, optimization, geometry, and high performance computing. My technical experience, both as a software engineer and as a mathematics student has involved research in the areas of control theory, particle accelerators, biological modeling, and RF systems.
