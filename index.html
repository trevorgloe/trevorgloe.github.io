About Me

Hello! My name is Trevor Loe and I am a double major in Physics and Mathematics at The California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. I have experience in programming computational math and physics (in python, matlab and fortran), control theory, and computational physics research. I have had to oportunity to work on several research projects relating to computational accelerator physics, optimal control theory, biology and areospace engineering. I am interested in research on computational mathematics of nonlinear systems, especially physically motivated systems and optimal control theory.
