Activities

Below are the conferences and presentations I have given related to research done.

Presentations

  • CSU Research Competition, SDSU - Chosen from the Cal Poly iternal competition to compete in the state-wide competition at SDSU. Presented a 10 minute presentation on research in Crab Cavity LLRF design and simulation work. Given on April 29th, 2023.
  • CSU Research Competition, Cal Poly SLO - Presented a 10 minute presentation on research in Crab Cavities LLRF design and simulation.
  • Brookhaven National Lab EIC Simulation Results - Contributed to presentation given to Brookhaven National Lab on results of EIC transverse stability studies. Presentation was given by Dr. Themis Mastoridis

Poster Presentations

  • MAA Mathfest, Philadelphia, PA - Presented poster "Applying Optimal Control with SQP to Cycling Performance Represented by Constituent Course Elements" in undergraduate poster session. Poster was presented on August 5th, 2022 and won "outstanding poster" designation from the conference.
  • APS Far West Section Meeting, Honolulu, HI - Presented "Crab Cavity Low-Level RF Design Simulation Tools for the Electron-Ion Collider" in poster session. Poster was presented on October 7th, 2022 and won the SPS poster award at the competition.
  • SULI Presentation, Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory - Presented "Beam Optics Analysis from the Mu2e External Line Commissioning" from work done over my 10 week SULI internship. Poster was presented on August 10th, 2022.

Other Conferences
